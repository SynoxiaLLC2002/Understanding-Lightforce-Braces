What Are Lightforce Braces?

LightForce braces are a revolutionary leap in orthodontic care. It uses both digital planning and cutting-edge 3D-printing technology to offer a highly effective treatment experience. Lightforce braces differ from traditional braces. These braces use digital models created through digital scans and eliminate the need for uncomfortable teeth impressions.

Using the digital scans, Lightforce braces ensure an exact match to the unique shape and size of each tooth. It further offers a more personalized orthodontic solution and enhances people’s smiles. Another thing that Lightforce braces use is advanced 3D-printing technology to create custom braces for your teeth.

What Does The Lightforce System Consist of?

  • Cloud Brackets: This is a type of 3D-printed ceramic bracket and is uniquely tailored for each patient. These brackets are smaller than traditional braces and are crafted using a clear material. These brackets are an ideal choice because they are comfortable to wear.
  • LightTrays: These trays are ergonomically designed and customized as per the needs of each patient. They play a crucial role in exact bracket placement which ensures optimal effectiveness. It is crafted from comfortable and lightweight clear plastic.
  • Light Brackets: This type of bracket is suitable for those seeking an aesthetic option that seamlessly blends with their natural tooth color. These braces take the invisibility game a step further. They are constructed from a clear, plastic material, which makes them almost invisible.
  • LightPlan: This is a top-tier digital treatment planning software that is developed by LightForce. This software empowers orthodontists to create, approve, and adjust personalized treatment plans for patients.

To sum it up, the Lightforce system leverages cutting-edge 3D-printing technology and covers a range of components like Light Brackets, Cloud Brackets, LightTrays, and LightPlan. This holistic approach ensures a highly customized, comfortable, and efficient orthodontic experience for patients.
